Purpose of the Chamber of Commerce, in Haitis Towns


The Chamber of Commerce in Haiti focuses on promoting business activities in Petionville and Delmas. Its primary objective is to stimulate the economy attract businesses and residents create opportunities and provide assistance to visitors. Unlike an organization it is not a branch of the government but collaborates with various governmental entities. The chamber is committed, to supporting businesses and individuals who aim to make a difference in their communities.
