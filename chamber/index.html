Purpose Of Chamber Of Commerce Town Haiti


The Chamber of Commerce Haiti focuses on promoting business in Petionville and Delmas. Its main goal is to boost the local economy, attract more businesses and residents, provide opportunities, and assist visitors. Unlike a social organization, it isn't a local government branch but collaborates with various governmental entities. The chamber is dedicated to working for local businesses and individuals who want to make a positive impact in their communities
